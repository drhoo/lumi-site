What does “foo” mean?

Foo can mean anything — hello, yes, no, oops, thank you! Kids naturally understand it. It’s safe, silly, and universal.

For grown-ups

The Lumi star tag is a gentle safety tag for school bags and kids’ items. No personal information is stored on the tag. Scans only show a simple return message. Your child’s identity stays private.

Lumi.foo never collects data or tracks anyone. It’s just a friendly place to meet Lumi and learn what “foo” can mean.
